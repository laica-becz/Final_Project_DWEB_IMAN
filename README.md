**Community Portal - Management System**
		
		A web-based community management system built with PHP, MySQL, and Apache — fully Dockerized for easy setup and deployment.

**About the System**

The Community Portal is a management system designed for barangay/community administrators and members. 
It allows administrators to manage announcements, respond to reports, update trash schedules, and oversee community operations. 
Community members can access announcements, view schedules, and submit reports or concerns.

**Features**

Administrator

- Secure login with role-based access control
- Manage community announcements (Create, Edit, Delete)
- Respond to member reports and concerns
- Manage trash collection schedules
- View and manage contact information
- Auto session timeout after 2 hours of inactivity

Community Member

- View community announcements
- Submit reports and concerns
- View trash collection schedules
- View community guidelines and contact info
